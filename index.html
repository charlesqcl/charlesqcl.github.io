Intro

I love cities. My work explores the relationship between humans and their environments across many dimensions - ecological, economic, and aesthetic. I‚Äôm drawn to the nature, science, and sociopolitics that shape how we live together in space.

Two ideas guide me. The first is understanding the sciences of how people interact with their surroundings. The second is exploring how the laws that govern cities and our responses to them can be reimagined to create more livable urban futures.

I use mixed methods. I use causal data science and machine learning not just as tools, but as languages for storytelling - ways to uncover meaning in complexity. I love working with people from different fields, where ideas collide and insights emerge. Curiosity keeps me moving; knowledge keeps me inspired.
